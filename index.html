FAQ & Pricing

How commissions work, what kind of projects are possible, and transparent prices in both Robux and USD.

What kind of work can you do?
Roblox scripting, game systems, UI with tweens, and F3X-based environments. Small prototypes or polishing existing projects are all possible.
Do you take both small and bigger projects?
Yes. Short scripts, UI tweaks, and compact maps are welcome, as well as more complete systems, as long as the scope is realistic for one developer.
Can a project be free?
If you DM the idea, and the project sounds interesting, clear, and not too time consuming, there is a chance it can be done for free.
How long does a project usually take?
Smaller tasks can be finished in a few days. Bigger systems or detailed environments usually take one to three weeks depending on complexity.
